on how to hone your music making skills and get a crowd moving!

  • Register: sign up here or using the link above

This course will be split up into three main categories: Content, Audience, and Business.
All three categories are explained below:




CONTENT AUDIENCE BUSINESS
Every DJ needs to know how to get good music and mix it well. During this part of the course, students will learn how to mix music and add effects to make it sound good!

Specific techniques include:
  • Song Structure
  • Digital DJing Technology
  • Music Sourcing
  • Filtering
  • Music Theory
  • Beatmatching
  • Scratching
Students will learn to use VirtualDJ and use a Numark DJ controller. They will also learn how to set up a Behringer PA System and mixer with microphone. 		The best DJs know how to tailor their music selection and mixing to their audience. In this sections, students will learn how to figure out what the audience will enjoy, and how to best meet their needs. Specific techniques include:
  • Party Structure
  • Filtering
  • Set list
  • Requests
  • Motivators
  • Stage setup






 Knowing how to DJ will only take you so far. Students need to learn how to promote their brand and make money off of their skills! Topics discussed include:
  • Maintaining your brand as a DJ
  • Pricing and forms of payment
  • Communication with planners
  • Website Promotion
  • Social Media Promotion
